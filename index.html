Games I've Worked On

Soul Food

Soul Food is a game I finished for the Gentle Ghost Jam over the course of a month using Unreal Engine 4. You serve comfort food to sad, lonely ghosts, where the menu and ingredient options act as analogues for more traditional dialogue options.

I worked alone on all aspects of the game except for the sound and music, which was made by Jessica Boudreau.
